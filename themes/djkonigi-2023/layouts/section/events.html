Now booking mobile and events in bars, clubs, corporate events & private parties.

Konigi is an open format DJ and can play a wide range of music based on the event, and also provide sound and lighting services. At corporate parties, galas and private parties I play eclectic sets of pop, dance music, disco, R&B, soul, rock, etc. as the client or venue requests. In bars and clubs I play house music nights.

I also DJ a silent disco monthly in the Bay Area, and hosted a monthly DJ meetup in Marin County to showcase DJs.

Sound

For events, we can provide speakers and audio equipment (mixers, controllers, microphone and other hardware). Our house system can serve up to 500 people, and we can work with rental services to provide sound for larger venues, as needed.

Lighting

For indoor events, we can provide lighting packages for uplighting and wash lights.

Silent Disco

For private events, we can provide headphones and transmitters for silent discos up to 50 people. For larger events we can work with rental services as needed.

Off-Grid Parties

We have battery powered gear (speakers and dj equipment, beach dj booths) and have hosted parties on beaches and in parks. Let us bring your party outdoors!
